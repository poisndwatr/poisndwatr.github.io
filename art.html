About:

This is a collection of some of my various visual artworks. Below you will find photography and editing, collages, pictures of fiber arts, and other visual art projects. I am drawn to visual arts, and really all forms of creating, because I love the idea of finding something or making something where there previously was nothing in a sense. With photography you are capturing the beautiful or the ordinary, with editing you are making what you captured stand out, with collages you can tell a story, with fiber arts like crocheting you are creating something new out of yarn, and beyond those the possibilities seem endless.
